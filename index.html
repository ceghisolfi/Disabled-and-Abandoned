DISABLED & ABANDONED

The Series in Graphics

A yearlong Austin American-Statesman investigation revealed how decades of underfunding and lax oversight have turned a system meant to care for and protect more than 100,000 of Texas' residents with disabilities into a too-often violent and sometimes deadly environment.

“Disabled & Abandoned” relied upon data-intensive research and on-the-ground reporting across Texas. Our investigative team:

Reviewed 10,000+ pages of investigative, legislative and regulatory reports
Collected and tabulated 2,000+ lawsuits alleging injustice, injury and death
Interviewed 100+ victims, attorneys, advocates and workers
Filed 50+ records requests to local, state and federal agencies
Traveled to 17 Texas counties to visit victims, workers and advocates
